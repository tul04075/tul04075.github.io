About Me

QUANQUAN YAN is a composer from Guangzhou China, who focuses on writing for solo instruments, chamber ensembles, orchestra, and Chinese folk instruments. He graduated from the Xinghai Conservatory of Music with a Bachelor of Composition and is currently pursuing his Master of Music in composition at Temple University studying withMatthew J.

Greenbaum and previously with Jan Krzywicki and William Dougherty. His works have been played and reading by Argus Quartet, Jasper Quartet and New Music Ensemble of Temple University

Also, in terms of music education, he has 5 years of experience in teaching piano and music theory, and the education targets are students of different levels from 6 to 20 years old. Served as a substitute teacher taught in Guangzhou Yu Yan High School and as art center teacher.
